Welcome to Sri Lanka

About the country

Sri Lanka is a tropical island nation in South Asia, located in the Indian Ocean. It is often called the “Pearl of the Indian Ocean” because of its natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. In the colonial era, the island went by the name Ceylon.


Read more about Sri Lanka

Famous Attractions

Sri Lanka is home to many beautiful and historic sites, including:

  • Ancient cities like Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa
  • Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Scenic tea plantations in the central highlands
  • Beautiful beaches along the coast
  • Wildlife sanctuaries and national parks

Culture and Cuisine

The people of Sri Lanka are known for their warmth and hospitality. Traditional festivals, colorful dances, and delicious cuisine like rice and curry, hoppers, and seafood are an important part of everyday life.
